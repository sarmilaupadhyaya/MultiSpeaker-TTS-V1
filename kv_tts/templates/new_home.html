Ki mo yê? | Who Am I?


Mô nom çé Raz Dent. Mo étidjé tèknòloji lingwistik é mo rès a Lafrans, mé mo té né é èlvé o Jorji.
3 zan pasé, mo komensé aprenn kréyòl kar famiy mô mær té sorti Lalwyizànn lontem pasé. Astœr, mo séyé kryé zouti pou sèrvi langaj kréyòl sur Internet.


I am a student in the Erasmus Mundus Master of Language and Communication Technology program. Originally from the state of Georgia, I completed the first year of my degree in Malta and am currently studying in Nancy, France. Some of my academic interests include automatic transliteration, low-resource speech processing, synthetic data, language ideology and language revitalization.
